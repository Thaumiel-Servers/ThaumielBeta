C.A.S.S.I.E

Discord Bot

The Central Automatic Service System for Internal Emergencies, also known as C.A.S.S.I.E or Cassie, is a bot for the Thaumiel Discord that Mr. Baguetter made.
Cassie manages things like mod-mail, tags, and member joining.
The name Cassie originates from the game SCP: Secret Laboratory, where it serves as a central computer for the facility.
There is a variant of Cassie in Thaumiel called Cassie AI that is based on GPT4o.
