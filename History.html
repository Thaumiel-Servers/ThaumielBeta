Thaumiel History

Founding of Thaumiel.

Thaumiel was founded on November 3rd, 2023. It was initially created to compete against the SCP: SL server, The Shy Guy One, before its untimely end. Thaumiel has replaced The Shy Guy One, with many of its old players now on its Discord and playing on its SL server.
