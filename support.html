Thaumiel Support

If you wish to contact support, you can email thaumiel.servers@gmail.com (Send Email) or join our Discord.

To report a staff member, you can email us with relevant proof of wrongdoing.
Alternatively, you can open a modmail ticket in our Discord and communicate with us that way.

Admin Abuse
  • Staff members are entrusted with certain powers to maintain order and enhance the gaming experience. These powers should be used judiciously and only for their intended purposes. Any form of admin abuse, including but not limited to, favoritism, targeting specific players without cause, or altering game outcomes unfairly, is strictly prohibited.
    • Game Integrity
  • Spawning waves of entities should occur naturally as part of the game's progression. Staff may only intervene to spawn waves during scheduled events or under exceptional circumstances that have been communicated to and agreed upon by the team. This ensures a fair and enjoyable experience for all players.
    • Confidentiality
  • Staff channels contain sensitive information that is crucial for the operation and integrity of the game. Leaking or sharing this information outside the authorized staff community undermines trust and can negatively impact the game environment. Staff members must keep all discussions and materials within staff channels confidential.
    • Transparency
  • Decisions made by staff that affect gameplay or players should be transparent and well-documented to maintain trust within the community.
    • Conflict Resolution
  • In the event of disputes among players or between players and staff, staff members should remain neutral and resolve issues based on facts and game rules. Personal biases should not influence the outcome.
    • Communication Standards
  • Staff should communicate in a professional and respectful manner at all times. This includes interactions with players and other staff members, both in-game and in official communication channels.
    • Fair Play Enforcement
  • Staff must actively monitor for and address any forms of cheating, hacking, or exploitation of game bugs. Players found violating fair play standards should be dealt with according to established protocols.
    • Privacy Protection
  • Staff must respect the privacy of all players. Do not share personal information of any player without their explicit consent.
    • Inclusivity
  • Promote an inclusive environment where all players, regardless of background, can enjoy the game. Discrimination of any kind will not be tolerated.
    • Player Harassment
  • Staff should take all claims of harassment seriously and investigate promptly. Ensure that the game is a safe space for all players.
    • VSR
  • All staff members must comply with the rules and guidelines established by the SCP: SL Verified Server Rules. This compliance is mandatory to maintain a consistent and fair gaming environment across the server. Failure to follow these rules may result in disciplinary action, up to and including removal from the staff team.
